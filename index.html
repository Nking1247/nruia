Why did I start Game Design?

  Much like many other kids my age, games have always been a part of my life. For me, it was a way to escape the hardships I encountered in my world. Fast forward to Sophomore year of high school. I hadn't played much video games, instead opting to take a larger part in the high school experience. However, Covid quickly derailed my plans and I found myself back in my room playing games. Among Us was the most popular game at the time and it was very simple. I figured that I could make something just as good. Additionally, I wanted to pay it forward to the next generation and provide them with games so they could escape their hardships.

Rush Rider

My First Fully Developed App! Rush Rider is now available on the App Store and the Play Store. Rush Rider is an endless runner like no other. The player swerves through traffic cones and cars on a bicycle. He is provided with full range of motion, but be careful! He is prone to tipping over if he leans too much. He can collect coins along the way to buy different bikes and outifts. Choose between City mode and Mountain mode.
