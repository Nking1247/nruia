Why did I start Game Design?

  Much like other kids my age, games have always been a part of my life. For me, games brought me closer to friends and distracted me from hardships in the real world. Fast forward to Freshman year of high school. I hadn't played many video games, instead opting to take a larger part in the high school experience. However, Covid quickly derailed my plans and I found myself back in my room playing games. Among Us was the most popular game at the time and it was very simple. I figured that I could make something just as good. Turns out, I really like Game Design. I hope to make a career out of giving other people the enjoyment that games gave me.
