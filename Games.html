Rush Rider

  My First Fully Developed App! Rush Rider is now available on the App Store and the Play Store. Rush Rider is an endless runner like no other. The player swerves through traffic cones and cars on a bicycle. He is provided with full range of motion, but be careful! He is prone to tipping over if he leans too much. He can collect coins along the way to buy different bikes and outifts. Choose between City mode and Mountain mode.

Space Game the Game

  A 3D vizualization of Space Jam. After learning more I embarked on a project to make a 3D version of the same game. This game provided many more challenges and was one of the first 3D games I made. I enjoyed scripting movement and other physics in 3D. In addition, I used scripting to randomly spawn the asteroids within the game, which was not easy. I am very proud of this game. Enjoy!

Stacker

  Stacker is an Arcade Game that I made for FrostHack, a local Hackathon for Game Designers. You have a limited number of blocks to shoot into your stack of blocks. Push your stack into the finish line before you run out of blocks. And make sure you avoid the magma cubes!

Tommy the Titan

  The one and only Tommy the Titan takes on a dangerous new challenge! Control Tommy as he leaps across death defying chasms. Careful of the cross bows aimed at Tommy's head, and make sure you don't get swept away in the massive tidal wave! This 2D platformer was made in honor of my school's mascot. It includes multiple levels and several types of obstacles to avoid. In this project I first learned how to animate.
